Please feel free to support us by joining our community forum and leave us feedback and suggestions.

We built our DKP system to work with the CT RaidTracker import of EQDKP. It might not be perfect but it 

worked with our DKP systems ;)

To automatically create raid event and notes you have to import the CTRT settings in the EQDKP Plus 

administration panel.
To do that, you simply have to login as an administrator and use the "CTRT Import" -> "Settings" menu.

Event Import:
-------------

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="ISO-8859-1" ?>
<EventTriggers>
	<EventTrigger>
		<trigger>Malygos</trigger>
		<result>Malygos</result>
	</EventTrigger>
	<EventTrigger>
		<trigger>Naxxramas</trigger>
		<result>Naxxramas</result>
	</EventTrigger>
	<EventTrigger>
		<trigger>Raid Start</trigger>
		<result>Raid Start</result>
	</EventTrigger>
	<EventTrigger>
		<trigger>Sartharion</trigger>
		<result>Sartharion</result>
	</EventTrigger>
</EventTriggers>
